Option #1 (1 thread):
    One thread does all the work.

Option #2 (2 threads):
    Two threads manage the filtering by checking 
    if the node exists in the opened list and 
    the closed list concurrently.

Option #3 (4 threads):
    Four threads manage the expansion of the 
    node and filter sequentially.

Option #4 (8 threads):
    Eight threads manage the expansion in 
    every direction and filtering is done 
    concurrenly.
